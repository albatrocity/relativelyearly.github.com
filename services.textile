h2. Spring Training <img src="/images/shoe.png" alt="Spring Training"/>

*A one-week planning and research period* to thoroughly map out your ideas and get them ready for a functioning application. A planning period will determine the following:

* *Usage Scenarios* What should happen during every step of interaction?
* *Monetization Strategies* How will you make money?
* *Market Research* What sets you apart?
* *Hosting Options* Where are you going to put this thing?
* *Interface Planning* How will your users interact?
* *Design Evaluation* Does it effectively communicate what you want it to?

        </div>
        <div class="col right">
          
h2. Extra Innings <img src="/images/scoreboard.png" alt="Extra Innings"/>

*On-going or short term development* of a new or existing project. Extra Innings are intended to provide the missing pieces, including but not limited to:

* Ruby on Rails feature development and implementation
* Transforming a concept design into valid XHTML/CSS
* API Integration/Development
* Code Review/Audits

        </div>
        <br class="clear"/>
          </div>

h2. Opening Day <img src="/images/field.png" alt="Opening Day"/>

*A two week working period that produces a fully-functional beta* of your application. Spring Training is required for an Opening Day product launch. Includes support and bugfixes for the following month.

        </div>        
        <div class="col right">

h2. Design <img src="/images/glove.png" alt="Design"/>

*Custom website and web application design*. Visual impact and user interfacing is an extremely important part of your online identity.

* Web/Visual Interface Design
* User Experience/Usability development and consulting
* Custom WordPress/Other CMS templates
        </div>
