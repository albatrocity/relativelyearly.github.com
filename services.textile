h2. Spring Training <img src="/images/shoe.png" alt="Spring Training"/>

A one-week planning and research period to thoroughly map out your ideas and get them on track for a functioning application. A planning period will determine the following:

* *Usage Scenarios* What should happen during every step of interaction?
* *Monetization Strategies* How will you make money?
* *Market Research* What sets you apart?
* *Hosting Options* Where are you going to put this thing?
* *Interface Planning* How will your users interact?
* *Design Evaluation* Does it effectively communicate what you want it to?
